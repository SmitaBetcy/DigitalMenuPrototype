To know about our history

From home page, please click on "Hear our Story".
From any other page, please click the top most centre button. This will display a small popup. Please, select "About Us" option to know about us

To view the list of menus available

From home page, please click on any of the categories you like from 'Today's Menu' and you will be able to view the list of menu's for that category.
For example, you can click on "Appetizers" and you will be able to view the list of menu's under appetizer.
From any other page, please click on top most left button and it will list the categories from which you might want to select the menus

To add Orders

Click on the specific category you like. Once the menu's are listed, click on 'add to order' and the quantity will be placed.
To add one more quantity, click on "add to order' again.

To reviews Orders

Please, click on top most left button and click on 'Place the order'. New page will be displayed with all your orders added. You can review them
